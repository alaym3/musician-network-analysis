'''Charles White Jr.''' (born August 2, 1994), known online as '''Cr1TiKaL''' ({{IPAc-en|Àà|k|r|…™|t|…™|k|…ôl}} {{respell|KRIT|ih|k…ôl}}), is an American [[YouTuber]] and [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] [[Online streamer|streamer]]. He became popular for his [[Let's Play|video game commentary]] videos on his "penguinz0" [[YouTube]] channel, mainly due to his monotone voice and deadpan delivery of humorous commentary. His comedic commentary videos have established him as a well-known and well-respected personality within the YouTube community.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Turner|first=Thomas|date=2018-08-18|title=3 Hilariously Creative Gaming YouTube Channels|url=https://studybreaks.com/tvfilm/gaming-channels-funny-creative/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210727023154/https://studybreaks.com/tvfilm/gaming-channels-funny-creative/|archive-date=July 27, 2021|access-date=2021-07-27|website=[[Study Breaks|Study Breaks Magazine]]|language=en-us|publication-place=[[University of Rhode Island]]|quote=}}</ref>

In 2018, White began [[livestreaming|streaming live]] on Twitch, where he mainly streams gaming content. White co-owns Human Media Group, a [[multi-channel network]] that serves online content creators and entertainers.<ref name="Hale2020">{{cite web|last=Hale|first=James|date=September 2, 2020|title=YouTube Reverses MoistCr1TiKaL, Markiplier Community Guidelines Strikes, Says They Were "An Over-Enforcement Of Policies"|url=https://www.tubefilter.com/2020/09/02/markiplier-moistcr1tikal-youtube-community-guidelines-strikes-road-rage/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210124141912/https://www.tubefilter.com/2020/09/02/markiplier-moistcr1tikal-youtube-community-guidelines-strikes-road-rage/|archive-date=January 24, 2021|work=[[Tubefilter]]|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref>

As of November 2021, White's YouTube channel has gained over 4.3&nbsp;billion video views and over 9&nbsp;million subscribers;<ref name="ytsb">{{cite web|url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/user/penguinz0|title=penguinz0's YouTube Stats|website=[[Social Blade]]|accessdate=April 24, 2021|archive-date=March 14, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210314033102/https://socialblade.com/youtube/user/penguinz0|url-status=live}}</ref> his Twitch channel has gained over 3&nbsp;million followers and over 53 million views.<ref name="twsb">{{cite web|title=moistcr1tikal's Twitch Stats Summary Profile|url=https://socialblade.com/twitch/user/moistcr1tikal|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210121072512/https://socialblade.com/twitch/user/moistcr1tikal|archive-date=January 21, 2021|website=[[Social Blade]]|accessdate=April 24, 2021}}</ref>

== Internet career ==

=== Early years and content evolution (2006‚Äì2017) ===
White started making YouTube videos in 2006, when he uploaded music and [[anime music video|AMVs]] to his old channels.<ref>{{Cite AV media|author=White, Charles, Jr.|publisher=penguinz0|title=Youtube Owes Anime Fans|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=26d5-dLklLU|via=[[YouTube]]|language=en|access-date=March 12, 2021|archive-date=January 21, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210121141616/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=26d5-dLklLU|url-status=live}}</ref><ref name="10Years" /> He created his penguinz0 YouTube channel on May 7, 2007.<ref>{{cite web|title=penguinz0 ‚Äì YouTube about page|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/penguinz0/about|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210111055947/https://www.youtube.com/user/penguinz0/about|archive-date=January 11, 2021|website=[[YouTube]]|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref> On August 5, he uploaded his first non-deleted video, which featured gameplay of ''[[Gears of War (video game)|Gears of War]]''.<ref>{{cite AV media|author=White, Charles, Jr.|publisher=penguinz0|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-FoH_02Icjk&feature=emb_title|title=gears of war jetpack glitch (NO Standbye or lag switch) AP|via=[[YouTube]]|date=August 5, 2007|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref> During this time, he streamed live on [[Justin.tv]] from late 2009 to early 2010.<ref>{{Cite AV media|author=White, Charles, Jr.|publisher=penguinz0|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?&v=ZlXXNxpCUqk&t=222s|title=My Past|via=[[YouTube]]|date=January 24, 2021|access-date=January 25, 2021|archive-date=May 24, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210524230420/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZlXXNxpCUqk&t=222s|url-status=live}}</ref>

White's audience rapidly grew after he uploaded a video titled ''The Most Difficult Game Ever Created Gameplay and Commentary'', in which he commentates while playing the online game ''[[QWOP]]'' and calls it "the hardest game he had ever played". The video gained popularity after [[Ray William Johnson]] reviewed it on his series ''Equals Three'', in which he praises White's sense of humor.<ref>{{cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?&v=0i2zaDSXhFM|title=GOOFY RUN (2010)|date=December 6, 2010|author=[[Ray William Johnson|Johnson, Ray William]]|type=video|time=2:21|access-date=January 19, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210524230407/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0i2zaDSXhFM|archive-date=May 24, 2021|url-status=live|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Hwang|first=Yeho|date=2016-03-21|title=QWOP Creator is NYU Professor|url=https://nyunews.com/2016/03/21/qwop-creator-is-nyu-professor/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-07-27|website=[[Washington Square News]]|quote=YouTube stars such as Ray William Johnson and popular television shows like ‚ÄúThe Office‚Äù have featured QWOP as well.|archive-date=July 27, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210727020450/https://nyunews.com/2016/03/21/qwop-creator-is-nyu-professor/}}</ref> In 2013, White was cited by ''[[Tubefilter]]'' as panning YouTube's new comment system, which required a [[Google+]] account to leave comments on videos.<ref>{{cite web|last=Gutelle|first=Sam|date=November 11, 2013|title=Creators, 88,000 Users Speak Out Against New YouTube Comments|url=https://www.tubefilter.com/2013/11/11/youtube-comments-petition-hank-green-penguinz0-jawed-karim/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210124141909/https://www.tubefilter.com/2013/11/11/youtube-comments-petition-hank-green-penguinz0-jawed-karim/|archive-date=January 24, 2021|work=[[Tubefilter]]|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref>

In 2012, White expressed his desire to donate all of his YouTube earnings to charity.<ref>{{Cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lw64XXlGEhE|title=Charity|author=White, Charles, Jr.|date=January 18, 2012|type=video|publisher=penguinz0|access-date=August 27, 2021|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> Later that year, he announced he had signed a partnership with [[Disney Digital Network|Maker Studios]] and had set up a Twitter account to disclose his charity donations.<ref>{{Cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ijmjJdq6_IE|title=Charity 2|date=April 13, 2012|author=White, Charles, Jr.|type=video|publisher=penguinz0|access-date=August 27, 2021|via=[[YouTube]]}}</ref> On September 2, 2014, he made a video expressing his gratitude to his fandom for helping him reach the one-million-subscriber milestone and said he has donated over $50,000 from his YouTube earnings to charity.<ref name=":4" />

In 2015, after eight years of anonymity, White made his [[Snapchat]] username, "big_moist", public, revealing his face. He uploaded his first video showing his face on camera on March 5, 2017.<ref name=10Years>{{cite AV media|author=White, Charles, Jr.|publisher=penguinz0|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GOpTRLDDwYE|title=10 Years on Youtube|via=[[YouTube]]|date=March 5, 2017|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref>

In early 2015, White began speaking about various topics, including internet drama. He drew attention from media outlets for his commentary videos,<ref>{{cite web|last=Hernandez|first=Patricia|url=https://www.kotaku.com.au/2016/09/why-youtubers-are-freaking-out-about-money-and-censorship/|title=Why YouTubers Are Freaking Out About Money And 'Censorship'|work=[[Kotaku]]|date=September 2, 2016|accessdate=December 25, 2020|archive-date=January 19, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210119075925/https://www.kotaku.com.au/2016/09/why-youtubers-are-freaking-out-about-money-and-censorship/|url-status=live}}</ref> which were often of discourse about online platforms and considerably critical of YouTube's policies.<ref name=Krajewski>{{cite web|last=Krajewski|first=Jake|date=May 5, 2016|title=YouTube, Where's the Fair Use?|url=https://reporter.rit.edu/features/youtube-wheres-fair-use|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210121042311/https://reporter.rit.edu/features/youtube-wheres-fair-use|archive-date=January 21, 2021|work=[[Reporter Magazine (RIT)|Reporter Magazine]]|publisher=[[Rochester Institute of Technology]]|accessdate=December 25, 2020}}</ref><ref name="Hale2020" />

In December 2016, White began co-hosting ''The Official Podcast'' with fellow [[Internet celebrity|Internet personalities]] ZealotOnPC, Huggbees, and Kaya Orsan.<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Official Podcast #32: Frequently Asked Questions|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X99Tq4DnwVc|url-status=live|access-date=September 23, 2021|website=[[YouTube]]}}</ref>
