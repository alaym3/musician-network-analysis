'''Tiffany Ariana Trump''' (born October 13, 1993)<ref>{{Cite web|first=Ryan|last=Struyk|url=https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/donald-trumps-kids-eric-ivanka-miss-deadline-vote/story?id=38303943|title=Trump Kids Eric and Ivanka Miss Deadline to Vote in NY GOP Primary |publisher=[[ABC News]] |date=April 11, 2016|access-date=October 11, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171012105540/http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/donald-trumps-kids-eric-ivanka-miss-deadline-vote/story?id=38303943|archive-date=October 12, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref> is the fourth child of former U.S. president [[Donald Trump]], and the only child with his second wife, [[Marla Maples]].

==Early life and education==
Tiffany Ariana Trump was born on October 13, 1993, at St. Mary's Medical Center in [[West Palm Beach, Florida]], two months before her parents married.<ref>{{cite magazine |url= http://www.vanityfair.com/news/2016/12/inside-ivanka-trump-and-tiffany-trump-complicated-sister-act |title=Inside Ivanka and Tiffany Trump Complicated Sister Act |last=Ellison |first=Sarah |date=February 2017 |magazine=[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]] |access-date= December 23, 2016 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20161224142406/http://www.vanityfair.com/news/2016/12/inside-ivanka-trump-and-tiffany-trump-complicated-sister-act |archive-date=December 24, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref> She is Donald Trump's only child with his second wife, actress and television personality [[Marla Maples]], whom he married in December 1993.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1993-10-15/news/9310150237_1_marla-maples-donald-trump-baby |title=Tiffany Trump Greets Attention with a Snore |last=Singer |first=Glenn |date=October 15, 1993 |work=[[Sun-Sentinel]] |access-date= December 23, 2016 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20161224095105/http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1993-10-15/news/9310150237_1_marla-maples-donald-trump-baby |archive-date=December 24, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref> She was named after jeweler [[Tiffany & Company]]; her father purchased the [[air rights]] above the company's [[Tiffany & Co. flagship store|Fifth Avenue flagship store]] in the 1980s while building [[Trump Tower]] next door. Her parents divorced in 1999 after being separated for two years.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/stork-visits-donald-marla-article-1.2717344|title=The stork visits Donald & Marla|last=Stasi|first=Linda|date=October 14, 1993|work=[[New York Daily News]]| access-date=October 14, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161019003745/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/stork-visits-donald-marla-article-1.2717344|archive-date=October 19, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> She was raised by her mother in [[California]].<ref name=NYT-20161002>{{cite news |url= https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/02/fashion/tiffany-the-other-trump.html |title=The Other Trump |newspaper=[[The New York Times]] |first=Alessandra |last=Stanley |date=October 1, 2016 |access-date=May 6, 2017 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170323011559/https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/02/fashion/tiffany-the-other-trump.html |archive-date=March 23, 2017 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref name= Slate07202016>{{cite news |last=Graham |first=Ruth |date=July 20, 2016 |title= Tiffany Trump Sad, Vague Tribute to Her Distant Father |url= http://www.slate.com/blogs/xx_factor/2016/07/20/tiffany_trump_s_sad_vague_rnc_speech.html |newspaper=[[Slate (magazine)|Slate]] |access-date=July 24, 2016 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20160723074854/http://www.slate.com/blogs/xx_factor/2016/07/20/tiffany_trump_s_sad_vague_rnc_speech.html |archive-date=July 23, 2016 |url-status=live }}</ref>

She has three older half-siblings, [[Donald Trump Jr.|Don Jr.]], [[Ivanka Trump|Ivanka]] and [[Eric Trump|Eric]], from Donald Trump's first wife, [[Ivana Trump|Ivana]],<ref name="Who is Tiffany Trump">{{cite news| last1= Krieg| first1=Gregory|title=Who is Tiffany Trump?|url=http://www.cnn.com/2016/04/13/politics/who-is-tiffany-trump-donald-family-town-hall/|access-date=June 9, 2016| publisher=[[CNN]]|date=April 13, 2016| archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20180701222302/https://www.cnn.com/2016/04/13/politics/who-is-tiffany-trump-donald-family-town-hall/|archive-date=July 1, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine |magazine=[[Newsweek]] |title=Ivanka Trump and Donald Jr. Tried to 'Bump' Tiffany Out of Her Inheritance, According to Newly Released Recordings |date= September 25, 2017 |access-date= September 26, 2017 |first=Christianna |last= Silva |url= http://www.newsweek.com/ivanka-trump-and-don-jr-tried-bump-tiffany-out-her-inheritance-according-newly-670841 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170926002106/http://www.newsweek.com/ivanka-trump-and-don-jr-tried-bump-tiffany-out-her-inheritance-according-newly-670841 |archive-date=September 26, 2017 |url-status=live }}</ref> and a younger half-brother, Barron, from Trump's third wife, [[Melania Trump|Melania]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Winsor|first1=Morgan|title=5 Things to Know About Tiffany Trump| url=https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/things-tiffany-trump-ahead-republican-national-convention-address/story?id=40698192|access-date=January 29, 2017|work=ABC News|date=July 19, 2016| archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170202060435/http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/things-tiffany-trump-ahead-republican-national-convention-address/story?id=40698192|archive-date=February 2, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2011, Trump released a music single called "Like a Bird". She later told ''[[The Oprah Winfrey Show]]''<ref>{{cite web|title=Introducing Tiffany Trump|url=http://www.wherearetheynow.buzz/meet-tiffany-trump|access-date=June 29, 2016|website=wherearetheynow.buzz|publisher=[[The Oprah Winfrey Show]] clip, [[Harpo Productions]], Inc.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160722163450/http://www.wherearetheynow.buzz/meet-tiffany-trump|archive-date=July 22, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref> that she was evaluating whether to take her music career "to the next level as a professional".<ref>{{cite news|last1=Yousefi|first1=Ryan|date=April 15, 2016|title=Tiffany Trump Cannot Escape Her Pop Song|work=[[Broward New Times]]|url=http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/news/tiffany-trump-cannot-escape-her-pop-song-7717799|access-date=June 9, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160610091810/http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/news/tiffany-trump-cannot-escape-her-pop-song-7717799|archive-date=June 10, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

"Like a Bird" received little media attention after its appearance on ''The Oprah Winfrey Show''. Following Trump's speech at the 2016 Republican National Convention, the song received renewed public interest.{{citation needed|date=March 2021}}

In 2015, Trump worked as an intern for ''[[Vogue (magazine)|Vogue]]'', and, in 2016, modeled for an Andrew Warren fashion show during [[New York Fashion Week]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Leone Shewfelt|first1=Raechel|date=February 16, 2016|title=Donald Trump's Daughter Tiffany Makes Her New York Fashion Week Debut|work=[[Yahoo News]]|url=https://www.yahoo.com/celebrity/donald-trumps-daughter-tiffany-makes-her-new-york-193922297.html|access-date=June 9, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160808202156/https://www.yahoo.com/celebrity/donald-trumps-daughter-tiffany-makes-her-new-york-193922297.html|archive-date=August 8, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

===2016 and 2020 presidential campaigns===

Trump made numerous appearances during her father's [[Donald Trump presidential campaign, 2016|2016 presidential campaign]].<ref>{{cite news|last1=Triggs|first1=Charlotte|title=Marla Maples and Tiffany Trump Likely to Get Secret Service Detail Amidst Donald Trump's Presidential Campaign as Marla Says, 'I Always Knew' He Would Run|url=http://www.people.com/article/marla-maples-tiffany-trump-donald-trumps-campaign|access-date=June 9, 2016|work=[[People magazine]]|date=April 20, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160513120656/http://www.people.com/article/marla-maples-tiffany-trump-donald-trumps-campaign|archive-date=May 13, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>

She spoke at a number of in-person campaign events in the weeks before the election.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Klein |first1=Betsy |title=Tiffany Trump announces engagement on her father's final full day in office |url=https://www.cnn.com/2021/01/19/politics/tiffany-trump-michael-boulos-engagement/index.html |website=CNN |access-date=20 January 2021 |date=19 January 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
While studying at the [[University of Pennsylvania]], Trump met fellow student Ross Mechanic, with whom she was in a relationship from about October 2015 to March 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Schmidt|first=Ingrid|date=March 21, 2018|title=Tiffany Trump and Boyfriend Ross Mechanic Call It Quits|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/tiffany-trump-breaks-up-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-1096508|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180423081930/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/tiffany-trump-breaks-up-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-1096508|archive-date=April 23, 2018|access-date=July 21, 2020|website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Polus|first=Sarah|date=February 20, 2018|title=The social media signs that Tiffany Trump and boyfriend Ross Mechanic may have broken up|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/reliable-source/wp/2018/02/20/the-social-media-signs-that-tiffany-trump-and-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-may-have-broken-up/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191114215847/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/reliable-source/wp/2018/02/20/the-social-media-signs-that-tiffany-trump-and-boyfriend-ross-mechanic-may-have-broken-up/|archive-date=November 14, 2019|access-date=July 21, 2020|website=[[The Washington Post]]|language=en}}</ref>
