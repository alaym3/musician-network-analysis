'''''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan''''' is an American [[Television comedy|comedy]] television series created by [[Tyler Perry]] that premiered on [[Nickelodeon]] on February 29, 2020. The series stars Dylan Gilmer, Celina Smith, Hero Hunter, Jet Miller, Mieko Hillman, [[Aloma Lesley Wright]], and [[Carl Anthony Payne, II]].

== Premise ==
Young Dylan is an aspiring hip-hop artist. When his mother doesn't come home one day, his grandmother Viola moves him in with his uncle Myles and aunt Yasmine to have parental figures in his life. His style clashes with the styles of Myles' family.

== Cast ==

=== Main ===
* Dylan Gilmer as Young Dylan,<ref name="Casting" /> an aspiring hip-hop artist
* Celina Smith as Rebecca, Young Dylan's older cousin and Charlie's older sister
* Hero Hunter as Charlie, Young Dylan's cousin, who used to have an imaginary friend
* Jet Miller as Bethany, Rebecca's best friend and Young Dylan's love interest
* Mieko Hillman as Yasmine, Young Dylan's aunt, Charlie and Rebecca's mother
* [[Aloma Lesley Wright]] as Viola, Young Dylan, Charlie and Rebecca's grandmother
* [[Carl Anthony Payne, II]] as Myles Wilson,<ref name="Casting" /> Young Dylan's uncle, Charlie and Rebecca's father

=== Recurring ===

* Samuel Goergen as Booder

== Production ==
On October 2, 2019, it was announced on ''[[The Ellen DeGeneres Show]]'' that [[Nickelodeon]] and [[Tyler Perry]] were teaming up to create a [[comedy]] series about a 10-year-old rapper with a working title of ''Young Dylan''. Tyler Perry serves as writer for the series, while [[Tyler Perry Studios]] produces the series. The series stars Dylan Gilmer as Young Dylan.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/10/tyler-perry-nickelodeon-live-action-series-starring-10-year-old-rapper-young-dylan-gilmer-1202750118/|title=Tyler Perry & Nickelodeon Team on Live-Action Series Starring 10-Year-Old Rapper Young Dylan|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|author=Denise Petski|date=October 2, 2019|access-date=February 19, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2019/10/02/nickelodeon-and-tyler-perry-studios-partner-to-produce-brand-new-kids-and-family-series-starring-young-dylan-795304/20191002nickelodeon01/|title=Nickelodeon and Tyler Perry Studios Partner to Produce Brand-New Kids and Family Series Starring Young Dylan|publisher=[[Nickelodeon]]|via=[[The Futon Critic]]|date=October 2, 2019|access-date=February 19, 2020}}</ref> On January 23, 2020, the cast for the series was announced. In addition to Dylan Gilmer, the cast also includes [[Carl Anthony Payne II]], Mieko Hillman, Celina Smith, Hero Hunter, and [[Aloma Wright]]. Tyler Perry also serves as executive producer and director for the series. Michelle Sneed serves as executive producer. Will Areu and Mark E. Swinton serve as producers.<ref name="Casting">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/01/tyler-perrys-young-dylan-nickelodeon-live-action-series-cast-1202838493/|title=''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan'' Nickelodeon Series Sets Cast|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|author=Denise Petski|date=January 23, 2020|access-date=February 19, 2020}}</ref> Jet Miller was also included in the principal cast as Rebecca's friend Bethany.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bet.com/celebrities/exclusives/tyler-perry_s-_young-dylan-cast-includes-hollywoods-new-rising-s.html|title=Tyler Perry's Young Dylan Cast Includes Some of Hollywood's Newest Rising Stars|work=BET|author=Alexis Reese|date=March 8, 2020|access-date=March 10, 2020}}</ref> On February 19, 2020, it was announced that ''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan'', previously ''Young Dylan'', would premiere on February 29, 2020. The series has been given a 14-episode order.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2020/02/nickelodeon-the-astronauts-loud-house-movie-big-nate-danger-force-spongebob-slate-1202862754/|title=''The Astronauts'', Live-Action ''Loud House'' Movie, ''Big Nate'' Series, ''Henry Danger'' Spinoff on Nick's Slate|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|author=Nellie Andreeva|date=February 19, 2020|access-date=February 19, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2020/02/19/nickelodeon-sets-premiere-date-for-tyler-perrys-young-dylan-474101/20200219nickelodeon05/|title=Nickelodeon Sets Premiere Date for Tyler Perry's ''Young Dylan''|publisher=[[Nickelodeon]]|via=[[The Futon Critic]]|date=February 19, 2020|access-date=February 19, 2020}}</ref>

On March 18, 2021, the series was renewed for a second season of 20 episodes,<ref name="Season 2 Renewal">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/03/danger-force-tyler-perrys-young-dylan-renewed-nickelodeon-side-hustle-1234716800/|title=''Danger Force'', ''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan'' Renewed by Nickelodeon; ''Side Hustle''{{'}}s Season Expanded|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|author=Denise Petski|date=March 18, 2021|access-date=March 18, 2021}}</ref> which premiered on June 12, 2021.<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2021/05/21/nickelodeons-danger-force-and-tyler-perrys-young-dylan-return-with-new-episodes-beginning-saturday-june-12-at-8-pm-et-pt-249110/20210521nickelodeon01/|title=Nickelodeon's ''Danger Force'' and ''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan'' Return with New Episodes Beginning Saturday, June 12, at 8 P.M. (ET/PT)|publisher=[[Nickelodeon]]|via=[[The Futon Critic]]|date=May 21, 2021|access-date=May 21, 2021}}</ref> On November 11, 2021, the series was renewed for a third season of 20 episodes.<ref name="Season 3 Renewal">{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2021/11/tyler-perrys-young-dylan-renewed-season-3-nickelodeon-1234872480/|title=''Tyler Perry's Young Dylan'' Renewed for Season 3 by Nickelodeon|work=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|author=Nellie Andreeva|date=November 11, 2021|access-date=November 11, 2021}}</ref>
