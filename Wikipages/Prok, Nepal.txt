'''Prok''' is a [[Village development committee (Nepal)|village development committee]] in [[Gorkha District]] in the [[Gandaki Zone]] of northern-central [[Nepal]]. At the time of the [[1991 Nepal census]] it had a population of 739 and had 219 houses in the village.<ref>{{Cite web| url=http://www.digitalhimalaya.com/collections/nepalcensus/form.php?selection=1|title=Nepal Census 2001|work=Nepal's Village Development Committees|publisher=[[Digital Himalaya]]|accessdate=2008-08-31}}</ref>
