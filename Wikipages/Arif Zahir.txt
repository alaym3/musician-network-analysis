'''Arif Zahir Lopes-Thrower''' (born April 15, 1994) is an American actor, rapper, singer, songwriter, [[Impressionist (entertainment)|voice impressionist]], and [[internet personality]] also known by his online alias '''Azerrz''' and his stage name '''4rif'''.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=4rif's YouTube Stats (Summary Profile) â€“ Social Blade Stats|url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/channel/UCx4009c0ifgDZ7qoG01wLMQ|access-date=September 25, 2020|website=socialblade.com}}</ref> His YouTube channel features voice impressions of a plethora of celebrities and cartoon characters with 6+ million subscribers. <ref name=":7">{{Cite web|last=Schneider|first=Michael|date=September 25, 2020|title='Family Guy' Casts YouTube Star Arif Zahir as the New Voice of Cleveland Brown|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/family-guy-cleveland-arif-zahir-1234783294/|access-date=November 10, 2020|website=Variety}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Moore|first=Camille|date=October 2, 2020|title=10 Things You Didn't Know about Arif Zahir|url=https://www.tvovermind.com/arif-zahir/|access-date=December 20, 2020|website=TVOvermind|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=September 25, 2020|title=Family Guy Finds New Cleveland Voice Actor with YouTube Star Arif Zahri|url=https://collider.com/family-guy-new-cleveland-voice-actor-arif-zahir/|access-date=December 20, 2020|website=Collider|language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2020, Zahir was selected to replace [[Mike Henry (voice actor)|Mike Henry]] as the voice of [[Cleveland Brown]] on the television series ''[[Family Guy]]''.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last1=Schneider|first1=Michael|date=September 25, 2020|title='Family Guy' Casts YouTube Star Arif Zahir as the New Voice of Cleveland Brown|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/family-guy-cleveland-arif-zahir-1234783294/|access-date=September 25, 2020|website=Variety|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|author=Leah Asmelash|title='Family Guy' finds new voice for Cleveland Brown|url=https://www.cnn.com/2020/09/25/entertainment/family-guy-cleveland-brown-arif-zahir-trnd/index.html|access-date=September 25, 2020|website=CNN}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title='Family Guy' casts YouTube star Arif Zahir as Cleveland Brown|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/pop-culture/tv/family-guy-casts-youtube-star-arif-zahir-cleveland-brown-n1241135|access-date=September 25, 2020|website=NBC News|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=YouTuber Arif Zahir Cast as Cleveland's New Voice on 'Family Guy'|url=https://www.tmz.com/2020/09/26/youtuber-arif-zahir-cast-cleveland-brown-family-guy-voice-black/|access-date=September 26, 2020|website=TMZ|language=en}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Zahir is of African-American and Cape Verdean descent and was born on April 15, 1994, in [[Berkeley, California]]. He was partially raised in [[New Bedford, Massachusetts|New Bedford]], [[Massachusetts]] where he attended Nativity Preparatory School and Keith Middle School before moving back to California in 2007.<ref>{{Cite web|date=October 28, 2020|first=Phil|last=Paleologos|title=New Bedford Native the New Voice of Cleveland Brown [Phil-osophy]|url=https://wbsm.com/new-bedford-native-the-new-voice-of-cleveland-brown-phil-osophy/|access-date=November 10, 2020|website=1420 WBSM|language=en}}</ref>

Zahir began doing voice impressions in middle school to impress his classmates. He originally made a YouTube channel in 2007 which focused on performing magic tricks.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Arif Lopes's YouTube Stats (Summary Profile) â€“ Social Blade Stats|url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/user/magicmind0|access-date=December 16, 2020|website=socialblade.com}}</ref> After doing so and finding success he started attending [[Hussian School of Art|Hussian College's]] campus in Los Angeles. He later dropped out to spend more time focusing on his YouTube career.<ref name="variety.com">{{Cite web|last1=Schneider|first1=Michael|date=October 14, 2020|title=How 'Family Guy' Newcomer Arif Zahir Landed His Big Break as the New Cleveland Brown|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/family-guy-cleveland-brown-arif-zahir-1234804840/|access-date=October 19, 2020|website=Variety|language=en}}</ref>
