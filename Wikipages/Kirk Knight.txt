'''Kirlan Labarrie''' (born November 16, 1995), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Kirk Knight''', is an American [[record producer]], rapper and member of the [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] collective [[Pro Era]]. His debut project, ''[[Late Knight Special]]'', was released on October 30, 2015. On July 21, 2017, he released a collaborative project with fellow Pro Era member Nyck Caution, ''Nyck @ Knight'', with its first single "Off the Wall" released on June 8, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://uk.complex.com/music/2017/06/kirk-knight-nyck-caution-announce-nyck-at-knight-project-drop-off-the-wall |title=
Kirk Knight And Nyck Caution Announce 'Nyck @ Knight' Project, Drop "Off The Wall" |date=June 8, 2017 |first=Edwin |last=Ortiz |work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]] |accessdate=July 12, 2017}}</ref> Kirk Knight is both a producer as well as a rapper. Kirk has produced top songs such as Flem by A$AP Ferg and Big Dusty by Joey Bada$$.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/KirkKnight/profile/|title=Kirk Knight|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=2018-05-03}}</ref> His debut studio album, Late Night Special, peaked on the Billboard charts on November 21, 2015 at number 33. Lasting one week on the charts.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/kirk-knight/chart-history/r-b-hip-hop-albums/song/952598|title=Kirk Knight Late Knight Special Chart History|work=Billboard|access-date=2018-05-03}}</ref> Recently, Kirk Knight as well as Joey Bada$$ of the Pro Era collective rap group have made hints at a new solo album coming out in 2018 for Kirk Knight.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/kirk-knights-new-album-is-coming-soon-according-to-joey-badass-news.48989.html|title=Kirk Knight's New Album Is Coming Soon, According To Joey Bada$$|work=HotNewHipHop|access-date=2018-05-03}}</ref> Kirk Knight also went on tour in 2018 with rap collective Flatbush Zombies along with fellow Pro Era member Nyck Caution.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://theproera.com/tour/kirk-knight/|title=Kirk Knight - Tour - Pro Era|work=Pro Era|access-date=2018-05-03|language=en-US}}</ref>
