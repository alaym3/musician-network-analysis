'''Yoasobi''' (stylized in [[all caps]]) is a Japanese music duo that was formed and debuted in 2019 under [[Sony Music Entertainment Japan]], composing of [[Vocaloid]] ([[Speech-generating device|voice synthesizer software]]) producer and songwriter [[:ja:Ayase|Ayase]], and singer-songwriter [[:ja:å¹¾ç”°ã‚Šã‚‰|Lilas Ikuta]]<ref name=lilas/> under the stage name Ikura. The duo has released songs based on [[short story|short stories]] posted on [[:ja:monogatary.com|Monogatary.com]], a website operated by their label, later also from various media like [[novels]], [[book]] tie-ups, and [[Letter (message)|letters]]. 

Yoasobi debuted with the single "[[Yoru ni Kakeru]]" in December 2019. It became successful on several music charts in Japan and gave them their breakthrough with charting atop for six weeks and 2020 year-end on the [[Billboard Japan Hot 100|''Billboard'' Japan Hot 100]]. It was followed by successful singles including "[[GunjÅ (song)|GunjÅ]]", and "[[Kaibutsu]]". Their debut extended play (EP) ''[[The Book (Yoasobi EP)|The Book]]'' was released in January 2021 and entered number two on both the [[Oricon Albums Chart]] and [[Billboard Japan#Albums|''Billboard Japan'' Hot Albums]].
