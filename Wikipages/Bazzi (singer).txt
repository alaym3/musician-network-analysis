'''Andrew Bazzi''' (born August 28, 1997), known mononymously by his surname, '''Bazzi''' ({{ipac-en|Àà|b|…ëÀê|z|i}} {{respell|BAH|zee}}<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sxLwWX2znFA|title=15 Things About 'Honest' Singer Bazzi You Should Know!|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|via=[[YouTube]]|date=April 16, 2018|access-date=May 6, 2020}} ''His name is pronounced at the start of the video.''</ref>),<ref name="BillMeet">{{cite news| last =Weatherby| first =Taylor| title =Meet Bazzi, the 20-Year-Old Rising Pop Star Who's Taking the Hot 100 by Storm With 'Mine'| work =Billboard| date =February 16, 2018| url =https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8100013/bazzi-interview-mine-hot-100-streaming| access-date =March 6, 2018| archive-date =March 31, 2019| archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20190331165752/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8100013/bazzi-interview-mine-hot-100-streaming| url-status =live}}</ref> is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer.<ref name="billboard debut">{{Cite magazine |last=Zellner |first=Xander |date=January 31, 2018 |title=Bazzi Makes Billboard Hot 100 Debut With 'Mine' |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8097385/bazzi-mine-hot-100-debut-streaming-numbers |magazine=Billboard |access-date=April 25, 2018 |archive-date=April 23, 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180423033143/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8097385/bazzi-mine-hot-100-debut-streaming-numbers |url-status=live }}</ref> His song "[[Mine (Bazzi song)|Mine]]", released in October 2017, gained popularity in early 2018 when it became a [[meme]] through [[Musical.ly]] edits, and the use of a [[Snapchat]] lens filter featuring the song. It peaked at number 11 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], and appeared on several international charts. He released his debut studio album, ''[[Cosmic (album)|Cosmic]],'' in 2018, which peaked at number 14 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] chart.

==Early life and education==
Bazzi was born on August 28, 1997, in the [[Detroit]] suburb, [[Canton, Michigan]].<ref name="iheart"/> His mother is American and his father is a [[Lebanese people|Lebanese]] immigrant.<ref>{{cite web|title=BAZZI|url=http://artistpg.com/partner/bazzi/|website=Artist Partner Group|access-date=August 17, 2020|archive-date=November 27, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201127135750/http://artistpg.com/partner/bazzi/|url-status=dead}}</ref> He learned to speak Arabic and play the Kazoo, [[oud]], and [[guitar]] as a child.<ref name="Apple">{{cite news| last =Yeung| first =Neil Z.| title =Bazzi on Apple Music| publisher =Apple Music| url =https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/bazzi/800263205| access-date =March 6, 2018| archive-date =May 2, 2019| archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20190502224432/https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/bazzi/800263205| url-status =live}}</ref><ref name="BWW">{{cite news| title =Bazzi Takes Over The Charts With MINE| work =Broadway World| date =February 14, 2018| url =https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Bazzi-Takes-Over-The-Charts-With-MINE-20180214| access-date =March 6, 2018| archive-date =June 12, 2018| archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20180612142531/https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Bazzi-Takes-Over-The-Charts-With-MINE-20180214| url-status =live}}</ref> In 2012, he began posting covers of songs on his [[YouTube]] channel. He attended [[Plymouth-Canton Educational Park]], where he also met Jeffrey Lu, Marc "Miller Time" Twinney, Chishti, Showbooo, Peter Bruchnak, and [[Rajiv Dhall]]. In November 2014, he moved to the [[Los Angeles]] area to pursue a music career. He finished high school at [[Santa Monica High School]] in 2015.<ref name="Samohi">{{cite news| last =Young| first =Nico| title =Andrew Bazzi| newspaper =The Samohi| date =March 27, 2015| url =http://www.thesamohi.com/ae/andrew-bazzi| access-date =March 6, 2018| archive-date =February 14, 2018| archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20180214073745/http://www.thesamohi.com/ae/andrew-bazzi| url-status =live}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Bazzi lives in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bazzi releases tribute - Music News - ABC News Radio|url=http://abcnewsradioonline.com/music-news/2020/4/1/bazzi-releases-tribute-to-girlfriend-of-two-years-renees-son.html|access-date=2021-01-25|website=abcnewsradioonline.com|language=en}}</ref> He has talked openly about his struggles with mental health and substance abuse while quarantining during the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Freedman|first=Adrianna|date=2020-05-18|title=This Is How Bazzi Is Taking Care of His Mental Health in Quarantine|url=https://www.menshealth.com/entertainment/a32581394/bazzi-music-mental-health-chat/|access-date=2021-01-25|website=Men's Health|language=en-US}}</ref> In an open letter on Twitter, he stated that he "let a drug problem get out of hand, I've been drinking my boredom away."<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bazzi Admits to Letting 'Drug Problem Get Out of Hand' amid Pandemic: 'I've Been Very Unhappy'|url=https://people.com/music/bazzi-admits-to-drug-problems-during-quarantine/|access-date=2021-01-25|website=PEOPLE.com|language=EN}}</ref> He is currently dating Renee Herbert and dedicated "Renee's song" to her.
