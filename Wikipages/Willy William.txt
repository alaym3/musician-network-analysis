'''Willy William''' (born 14 April 1981) is a French DJ, record producer and singer, famous for his club remixes and vocal collaborations with a number of dance music artists. He is most famous for his French hit "[[Ego (Willy William song)|Ego]]" and his Latin hit "[[Mi Gente (J Balvin and Willy William song)|Mi Gente]]", the latter with [[J Balvin]].

==Early life==
Born to immigrant parents in 1981, in [[Fréjus, Var]], France,<ref>{{cite web|last1=Buchanan|first1=John D.|title=Willy William – Biography|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/willy-william-mn0001597262/biography|website=[[AllMusic]]|publisher=[[RhythmOne]]|access-date=26 February 2018|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170925040444/https://www.allmusic.com/artist/willy-william-mn0001597262/biography|archive-date=25 September 2017}}</ref> he was brought up in the small town of [[Champagné, Sarthe|Champagné, Pays de la Loire]], in northwestern France.

== Career ==
He came to prominence in 2003 with his work with DJ Flex in the dancefloor hit "B Boyz Shake da Body" and in 2004 as producer of "Tragedie" using the pseudonym "Lord William".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.southindiescrew.com/?page=artiste&artiste=Willy%20William|title=South Indies Crew: Willy William biography}}</ref> He later joined the French music collective [[Collectif Métissé]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://le-collectif-metisse.skyrock.com/|title=Collectif Metisse|website=Skyrock}}</ref> also continuing to produce his own materials.

He collaborated with a great number of artists producing their songs and taking part in vocals as featured artists. He charted with collaborative songs with [[DJ Assad]] with "Li tourner", [[Les Jumo]] with "C Show" and "L'Italienne", and with [[Keen'V]] in "On s'endort?", [[Ridsa]] in "Je n'ai pas eu le temps" that also featured [[Ryan Stevenson (drummer)|Ryan Stevenson]] on drums and many others.<ref name=bstars>{{Cite web|url=https://bstars.eu/item/willy-william-849.html|title=Booking Stars Ltd. Booking & Touring Agency. - Willy William|website=bstars.eu}}</ref>

In 2016, Willy got his major French hit with "Ego" which was produced and sung by himself. It was the main single from his 2016 album ''Une seule vie'' and his biggest French hit as a solo artist. "Ego" charted in France, Italy, Germany, Belgium and Poland amongst many others.<ref name=bstars/>

In 2017, he became an international sensation after the release of "[[Mi Gente (J Balvin and Willy William song)|Mi Gente]]" a collaboration with the superstar Colombian artist [[J Balvin]]. The huge international success of the song led to a version that also featured [[Beyoncé]] in a new remix. The song received a [[Latin Billboard Music Awards|Latin Billboard Music Award]] and the music video amassed over 2 billion views and received in 2018 an [[MTV Video Music Award]] for Best Latin Video, maintained #1 Spotify Global for 12 consecutive weeks, while topping charts across the world as #1 in a great number of countries.

In 2018, Willy produced and released a dance track called "La La La" and he appeared on "[[Goodbye (Jason Derulo and David Guetta song)|Goodbye]]", a [[David Guetta]] and [[Jason Derulo]] song that featured William and [[Nicki Minaj]].

Willy William is a "Latin Grammy" nominated DJ / Producer mainly for "Mi Gente". His song "Ego" made him the #1 most Shazamed French Artist.
