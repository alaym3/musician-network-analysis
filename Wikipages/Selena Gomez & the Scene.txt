'''Selena Gomez & the Scene''' was an American [[pop rock]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fusion.net/video/224314/selena-gomez-revival-interview-2015/|title=Selena Gomez talks Revival and what it's like to be a boss|work=[[Fusion (TV channel)|Fusion]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bustle.com/articles/108430-selena-gomez-talks-new-album-revival-it-sounds-like-it-could-be-her-most-personal|title=Selena Gomez Talks New Album 'Revival' & It Sounds Like It Could Be Her Most Personal Effort Yet
|work=[[Bustle (magazine)|Bustle]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/2295623/selena-gomez-kmart-clothing-collection/|title=5 Pieces We Want From Selena Gomez's 'Dream Out Loud' Kmart Collection|work=[[MTV News]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://montrealgazette.com/entertainment/music/concert-review-selena-gomez-the-scene-bell-centre-october-30|title=Concert Review: Selena Gomez & the Scene, Bell Centre, October 30|author=News Desk More News Desk|work=[[Montreal Gazette]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|first= Emily |last= Goodman |title= Selena Gomez and the Scene rocked out together for more than three years |date= 12 November 2014 |publisher= [[AXS (ticket merchant)|AXS]] |url= http://www.axs.com/selena-gomez-and-the-scene-rocked-out-together-for-more-than-three-yea-27851 |access-date= 31 October 2015}}</ref> band from [[Hollywood, California]]. Formed in 2008, its last lineup consisted of lead vocalist [[Selena Gomez]], bassist Joey Clement, drummer Greg Garman, keyboardist Dane Forrest, and guitarist Drew Taubenfeld. The band released three [[studio album]]s, seven [[single (music)|singles]] and nine music videos.

Their debut album, ''[[Kiss & Tell (Selena Gomez & the Scene album)|Kiss & Tell]]'', was released on September 29, 2009, debuting at No. 9 on the US [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] and earning the band a Gold certification from the [[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA) in March 2010. The second single from the album, "[[Naturally (Selena Gomez & the Scene song)|Naturally]]", reached the top thirty in the U.S., as well as the top twenty in New Zealand, the United Kingdom, Ireland, Canada, and Germany. The song has been certified Platinum in the U.S. and Canada. As of 2012, ''Kiss & Tell'' has sold over 900,000 copies in the U.S.

The band's second album, ''[[A Year Without Rain]]'', was released on September 17, 2010, debuting at No. 4 on the U.S. ''Billboard 200'' and earning the band a second RIAA Gold certification in January 2011. Two singles were released from the album, "[[Round & Round (Selena Gomez & the Scene song)|Round & Round]]" and "[[A Year Without Rain (Selena Gomez & the Scene song)|A Year Without Rain]]". As of 2012, the album has sold over 800,000 copies in the U.S.

The band's third album, ''[[When the Sun Goes Down (Selena Gomez & the Scene album)|When the Sun Goes Down]]'', was released on June 28, 2011,<ref name="jsyk. com">{{cite web |url=http://www.jsyk.com/2011/05/18/selena-gomez-interview/ |title=Selena Gomez Talks About 'Who Says' and Her Upcoming Tour (Video) |author=Cheung, Nadine |date=2011-05-18 |work=Just So You Know |publisher=[[AOL|Aol, Inc.]] |access-date=2011-05-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120723021143/http://www.cambio.com/2011/05/18/selena-gomez-interview/ |archive-date=2012-07-23 |url-status=dead }}</ref> debuting at No. 4 on the U.S. ''Billboard 200''. Its lead single "[[Who Says (Selena Gomez & the Scene song)|Who Says]]" received its radio premiere on ''[[On Air with Ryan Seacrest (radio)|On Air with Ryan Seacrest]]'' on March 8, 2011, followed by a music video premiere on Disney Channel on March 11. The song has been certified Platinum in the U.S. The second single from the album, "[[Love You like a Love Song]]", was released on June 17, 2011 and was certified 4x Platinum in the U.S. The third and final single from the album was "[[Hit the Lights (Selena Gomez & the Scene song)|Hit the Lights]]".<ref name="ask billboard">{{cite web|first=Gary|last=Trust|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/472033/ask-billboard-from-sm-to-disney-acts|title=Ask Billboard: From 'S&M' To Disney Acts |work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=2011-04-15|access-date=2011-04-17}}</ref> The band disbanded when Gomez began focusing on her acting career and solo music career.

The band has won numerous awards and accolades since their debut. They have won a total of four Poptastic Awards, including the Best Duo or Group Award. The band has also been nominated for five awards at the [[MuchMusic Video Awards]],<ref>{{cite web|last=Iandoli |first=Kathy |url=http://idolator.com/6474141/2012-muchmusic-video-awards-justin-bieber-drake-carly-rae-jepsen |title=2012 MuchMusic Video Awards: Justin Bieber, Drake & Carly Rae Jepsen Score Nominations &#124; Music News, Reviews, and Gossip on |publisher=Idolator.com |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mmva.muchmusic.com/tag/selena-gomez/ |title=Selena Gomez &#124; 2014 MMVAs |publisher=Mmva.muchmusic.com |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref> and two [[MTV Europe Music Awards]].<ref>{{cite web|first=Chris |last=Rogers |url=http://hollywoodlife.com/2012/09/17/ema-nominees-2012-nominations-mtv-europe-music-awards/ |title=2012 MTV Europe Music Awards Nominations — FULL List Of EMA Nominees |publisher=Hollywood Life |date=2012-09-17 |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/2012/09/17/mtv-europe-music-awards-nominations-rihanna_n_1889434.html |title=MTV Europe Music Awards: Rihanna Leads Nominations With Six Nods |publisher=Huffingtonpost.co.uk |date=2012-09-17 |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref> In 2011, the band won its first [[People's Choice Award]] for Breakout Group. In 2012, "Love You Like a Love Song" was nominated for an [[MTV Video Music Award]], giving the band their first VMA nomination.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1690827/video-music-awards-nominees-list-2012.jhtml |title=2012 VMA Nominations: The Complete List |publisher=MTV |date=2012-07-30 |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=April |last=Chieffo |url=http://thecelebritycafe.com/feature/2012/07/selena-gomez-scores-her-first-vma-nomination |title=Selena Gomez scores her first VMA nomination |publisher=TheCelebrityCafe.com |date=2012-07-31 |access-date=2015-02-19 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141107185511/http://thecelebritycafe.com/feature/2012/07/selena-gomez-scores-her-first-vma-nomination |archive-date=2014-11-07 }}</ref> The band has also won a total of six [[Teen Choice Awards]] since 2010, including Choice Music: Group and Choice Music: Love Song for "Love You like a Love Song".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1411125/awards|title=Selena Gomez: Awards|publisher=IMDb.com|access-date=19 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/8301-31749_162-57476951-10391698/teen-choice-awards-to-be-handed-out-this-weekend/ |title=Teen Choice Awards to be handed out this weekend |publisher=CBS News |date=2012-07-20 |access-date=2015-02-19}}</ref>

I think if anything the lyrics are more powerful, in a way."
