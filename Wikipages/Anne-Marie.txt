'''Anne-Marie Rose Nicholson''' (born 7 April 1991),<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.delitmusic.fr/eva/|language=fr|title=Anne-Marie|date=3 December 2018|publisher=DelitMusic|access-date=11 February 2021|archive-date=9 June 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210609105308/https://www.delitmusic.fr/eva/|url-status=live}}</ref> known [[Mononymous person#The%20West|mononymously]] as '''Anne-Marie''', is an English singer. She has attained charting singles on the [[UK Singles Chart]], including [[Clean Bandit]]'s "[[Rockabye (song)|Rockabye]]", featuring [[Sean Paul]], which peaked at number one, as well as "[[Alarm (Anne-Marie song)|Alarm]]", "[[Ciao Adios]]", "[[Friends (Marshmello and Anne-Marie song)|Friends]]", "[[2002 (song)|2002]]" and "[[Don't Play]]".<ref name="Spotify">{{cite web |url=https://www.msn.com/en-us/health/medical/anne-marie-performs-alarm-acoustic/vp-AAjsNNO |title=100 million streams on Spotify |website=[[MSN]] |access-date=27 October 2016 |archive-date=11 November 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161111200915/http://www.msn.com/en-us/health/medical/anne-marie-performs-alarm-acoustic/vp-AAjsNNO |url-status=live }}</ref> Her debut studio album, ''[[Speak Your Mind]]'', was released on 27 April 2018, and peaked at number three on the [[UK Albums Chart]].<ref name="SYM">{{cite web |url=https://www.facebook.com/iamannemarie/videos/1596996217015412/ |title=Anne-Marie |via=Facebook |access-date=22 February 2018}}{{Primary source inline|date=June 2020}}</ref> She was nominated for four awards at the [[2019 Brit Awards]], including Best British Female Solo Artist. In 2015, she signed a record deal with [[Asylum Records|Asylum]] (a sub-label of [[Atlantic Records]]) and began releasing her work through the label. Since 2021, she has been a coach on ''[[The Voice UK]]''.
