'''Cody Osagie''', (born October 1, 1994), better known by his stage name '''Cozz''', is an American rapper from [[Los Angeles]], [[California]]. He is signed to [[J. Cole]]'s label [[Dreamville Records]] and [[Interscope Records]]. His debut studio album ''[[Cozz & Effect]]'', was released on October 3, 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/06/interview-cozz-talks-j-cole-dreamville-interscope-deal-album|title=Interview: Cozz Talks About Signing to J. Cole's Dreamville/Interscope Label, Hanging Out With Game & His Upcoming Album "Cozz & Effect"|author=Edwin Ortiz|date=29 June 2014|work=Complex}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/10/cozz-cozz-and-effect-album-stream|title=Stream Cozz's "Cozz & Effect" Album|author=Edwin Ortiz|date=3 October 2014|work=Complex}}</ref>

==Musical career==
===2013-15: Career beginnings and ''Cozz & Effect''===
In 2013, Cozz began to take rap seriously, and recorded a demo tape. A few months later, he released the music video for "Dreams". Shortly after, he started to get notice, and having meetings with many different labels. Cozz's manager brought the music video to [[J. Cole]] and was later signed to [[Dreamville Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://saintheron.com/featured/interview-dreamville-crew-talks-about-upcoming-projects/|title=Interview: Dreamville Talks Upcoming Projects|work=Saint Heron}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ca.complex.com/music/2014/06/interview-cozz-talks-j-cole-dreamville-interscope-deal-album|title=Interview: Cozz Talks About Signing to J. Cole's Dreamville/Interscope Label, Hanging Out With Game & His Upcoming Album "Cozz & Effect"|author=Edwin Ortiz|date=29 June 2014|work=Complex CA}}</ref>

On October 3, 2014, Cozz released his debut album ''[[Cozz & Effect]]'' The project was initially intended as a free mixtape upon recording. But according to Cozz, when it was finished, it sounded better than they had hoped and they therefore agreed it can be sold. ''[[Dreamville Records]]'' released it on digital format firstly, followed by physical CD a short while afterwards. In 2015, Cozz was a part of [[J. Cole]]'s "[[2014 Forest Hills Drive|2014 Forest Hills Drive Tour]]" along with other acts [[Bas (rapper)|Bas]], [[Omen (musician)|Omen]], [[Pusha T]], [[Jhene Aiko]], [[Jeremih]], [[YG (rapper)|YG]], and [[Big Sean]].

===2016-present: ''Nothin' Personal'' and ''Effected''===
On January 4, 2016, Cozz released his second project, and first mixtape, ''[[Nothin' Personal (Cozz album)|Nothin' Personal]]''. The mixtape was entirely produced by Meez. Features include fellow west coast rapper [[Boogie (rapper)|Boogie]], Bas, and Free Ackrite.<ref>{{cite web|last=Hernandez |first=Victoria |url=http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.36785/title.cozz-announces-nothin-personal |title=Cozz "Nothin Personal" Mixtape Stream, Cover Art & Tracklist |publisher=HipHopDX |date=2016-01-05 |access-date=2016-04-02}}</ref> The mixtape was supported with four singles: "Tabs," "Growth," "My Side," and "Who Said."

On February 13, 2018, he released his second album ''[[Effected (album)|Effected]]'' which includes features from J. Cole and [[Kendrick Lamar]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8098068/cozz-effected-track-list|title=Cozz Enlists Kendrick Lamar & J. Cole for 'Effected': See Track List|date=February 5, 2018|website=Billboard}}</ref> Singles and music videos for the album includes "Questions", "Demons N Distractions", and "Bout It".<ref name="India">{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/video/2018/01/cozz-role-reversal-cops-questions-video/|title=Cozz Plays Role Reversal With Cops in ''Questions'' Video|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|last=India|first=Lindsey|date=January 8, 2018|access-date=February 12, 2018}}</ref><ref name="hotnewhiphop.com">{{cite web|last1=Findlay|first1=Mitch|title=Cozz & Garren Woo Ladies & Piss Off Executives In "Bout It"|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/cozz-and-garren-woo-ladies-and-piss-off-executives-in-bout-it-new-video.43781.html|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=May 23, 2018|date=May 22, 2018}}</ref> On April 4, 2018, Cozz announced The Effected Tour in North American in 16 cities, to further promote the album.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Walker|first1=Joe|title=Cozz Announces 'The Effected Tour'|url=http://onsmash.com/music/cozz-effected-tour-dates/|website=OnSmash|access-date=April 4, 2018|date=April 4, 2018}}</ref> He was also featured on Los Angeles rapper JAG's album with [[Reason (rapper)|Reason]] on the track "Black Boy Rise". On November 7, Cozz worked with DJ Megan Ryte on a 5-track mixtape called ''Aftermath Of My Dreams''.<ref>{{cite web |title=NEW MIXTAPE: DJ MEGAN RYTE & COZZ 'AFTERMATH OF MY DREAMS' |url=http://rapradar.com/2018/11/07/new-mixtape-dj-megan-ryte-cozz-aftermath-of-my-dreams/ |website=Rap Radar}}</ref>
