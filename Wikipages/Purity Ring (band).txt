'''Purity Ring''' is a Canadian [[electronic pop]] band from [[Edmonton]], [[Alberta]], Canada, formed in 2010. The band consists of multi-instrumentalist/producer Corin Roddick and vocalist Megan James. They released their debut album ''[[Shrines (Purity Ring album)|Shrines]]'' in 2012 to critical acclaim, followed by ''[[Another Eternity]]'' in 2015. The band released their third album, ''[[Womb (album)|Womb]]'', on April 3, 2020.
