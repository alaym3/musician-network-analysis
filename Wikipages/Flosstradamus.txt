'''Flosstradamus''' is the stage name of American DJ and musician [[Curt Cameruci]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://runthetrap.com/2018/09/25/listen-boombox-cartel-flosstradamus-id-collab/|title=LISTEN: Boombox Cartel & Flosstradamus Unleash Anticipated "ID" Collaboration|date=2018-09-25|work=Run The Trap: The Best EDM, Hip Hop & Trap Music|access-date=2018-10-19|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="USA Today">{{cite news|title=Lollapalooza 2011: Flosstradamus wants to have fun|newspaper=[[USA Today]]|url=https://www.usatoday.com/life/music/news/2011-07-26-flosstradamus-lollapalooza-2011_n.htm|access-date=18 July 2012|first=Victoria|last=Rodriguez|date=3 August 2011}}</ref> From 2007 until 2016, Flosstradamus consisted of Cameruci and Josh Young from [[Chicago]]. The duo announced they would both be pursuing solo careers, with Cameruci to continue using Flosstradamus as a stage name.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7632327/flosstradamus-split-josh-young|title=Flosstradamus' Josh Young Announces Split, Will Focus on Solo Project in 2017|last=Bein|first=Kat|date=20 December 2016|newspaper=Billboard|access-date=2016-12-22}}</ref> Cameruci is also known as Autobot and Young is also known as J2K and YehMe2.<ref name="billboard.com">{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7685954/josh-young-flosstradamus-yehme2-both-remix-interview|title=Josh Young Talks Leaving Flosstradamus for New Project YehMe2, Shares Remix of Gucci Mane and Drake's 'Both:' Exclusive}}</ref> Flosstradamus is known for being one of the early pioneers of the [[trap (EDM)|trap]] genre, made popular by their remix of [[Major Lazer]]'s hit single "[[Original Don]]".

Flosstradamus, as a duo, have worked alongside artists including [[Kid Sister]] (Young's sister), [[Diplo]], [[Three 6 Mafia]]'s [[Juicy J]], [[The Cool Kids]], and [[A-Trak]].<ref name=DJZ>{{cite web|title=Flosstradamus Bio, Music, News & Shows|url=http://djz.com/featured_djs/flosstradamus/|publisher=DJZ.com|access-date=February 28, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130329014145/http://djz.com/featured_djs/flosstradamus/|archive-date=March 29, 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref> They played at many college campuses such as [[Wellesley College]], [[Drexel University]] (alongside [[Waka Flocka Flame]]), [[Northwestern University]], [[Carnegie Mellon University]] (playing alongside [[RJD2]]), [[Middlebury College]], [[Columbia University]], and [[UC Riverside]]. They have additionally appeared on ''[[Late Night with Jimmy Fallon]]'', and the [[Nick Jr]]. program ''[[Yo Gabba Gabba!]]''.<ref name="USA Today" /><ref>{{cite web|title=Kid Sister Gives Us A "Right Hand Hi" With A Little Help From Flosstradamus|publisher=Late Night with Jimmy Fallon|url=http://www.latenightwithjimmyfallon.com/blogs/2009/11/kid-sister-gives-us-a-right-hand-hi-with-a-little-help-from-flosstradamus/|access-date=18 July 2012}}</ref>

==Career==
In April 2006, Flosstradamus, as a duo, were included in [[URB (magazine)|URB]]'s annual "Next 100".<ref>{{cite web|title=Flossin' at the top of our NEXT 100|publisher=URB|url=http://www.urb.com/2007/02/01/flossin-at-the-top-of-our-next-100/|access-date=18 July 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121001004943/http://www.urb.com/2007/02/01/flossin-at-the-top-of-our-next-100/|archive-date=1 October 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref> In addition, during the summer of 2007 they embarked on an international tour with [[Chromeo]], dubbed the "Fancy Footwork Tour". Autobot is also active in the Serato [[Scratch Live]] community making his Flossy FX sampler available online to the masses via their [[MySpace|MySpace page]]. In 2014, at SXSW Flosstradamus set up a guerrilla WiFi network titled PLURNTNET to distribute a new track titled "Hdygrlz Anthm". Using social media the duo pointed fans to the PLURTNET via geotagged posts at various locations around Austin. Users could sign on and download or stream the track.

On July 29, 2016, they released their single 'Came Up' with FKi 1st, graves and featuring [[Post Malone]] and Key!.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/came-up-feat.-post-malone/id1133289272|title=Came Up (feat. Post Malone & Key!) - Single by Flosstradamus, FKi 1st & graves on Apple Music|website=Apple|date=29 July 2016|access-date=2016-12-01}}</ref> In December 2016, Young announced Flosstradamus were splitting and he will pursue a solo career.<ref name=":0" /> Young's solo project soon adopted the name YehMe2.<ref name="billboard.com"/> Cameruci will continue with the name Flosstradamus as a solo act.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://edmchicago.com/2016/12/21/flosstradamus-will-continue-solo-act/|title=Flosstradamus Will Continue as a Solo Act|last=Sheban|first=Molly|date=2016-12-21|website=EDM Chicago|access-date=2016-12-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/news/id.41754/title.flosstradamus-splits-as-one-half-of-duo-leaves-to-pursue-solo-career|title=Flosstradamus Splits As One Half Of Duo Leaves To Pursue Solo Career|last=http://hiphopdx.com|first=HipHopDX -|newspaper=HipHopDX|language=en-US|access-date=2016-12-22}}</ref> In March 2017 he released the single "Back Again", which featured [[Waka Flocka Flame]] and Mayhem through Ultra Music.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7728570/flosstradamus-back-again-mayhem-waka-flocka-flame|title=Flosstradamus is 'Back Again' With Mayhem and Waka Flocka Flame: Listen|last=Bein|first=Kat|date=March 17, 2017|work=Billboard|access-date=2017-03-17}}</ref> They have also partnered with [[Dillon Francis]] to form the touring and live performance group Dillstradamus.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/11/15/edm-interview-flosstradamus-dillstradamus-hdynation-future-trap/|title=YOUR EDM INTERVIEW WITH FLOSSTRADAMUS, ON DILLSTRADAMUS, HDYNATION & THE FUTURE OF TRAP|author=MATTHEW MEADOW|date=November 15, 2016|publisher=Your EDM}}</ref>

== In popular culture ==
The musician collaborated with [[Hemper]] to create his own smoking accessory box, typically used to consume [[marijuana]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://dancingastronaut.com/2016/05/flosstradamus-hemper-contest/|title=Win the Flosstradamus HDY smoking experience box|date=2016-05-11|work=Dancing Astronaut|access-date=2018-10-01}}</ref> Other musicians such as [[Ty Dolla Sign|Ty Dolla $ign]], [[Fetty Wap]], and [[2 Chainz|2Chainz]] did the same along with Flosstradramus.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.greenentrepreneur.com/article/315374|title=Cannabis Boxes Curated by Celebrities Are Big Business|last=Staff|first=Green Entrepreneur|date=2018-07-05|work=GreenEntrepreneur|access-date=2018-10-01}}</ref>
