'''Thomas Abraham Misch''' (born 25 June 1995) is an English musician and producer. He began releasing music on [[SoundCloud]] in 2012<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfweekly.com/music/3-must-see-acts-this-week-wax-idols-king-krule-and-tom-misch/|title=Three Must-See Acts This Week: Wax Idols, King Krule, and Tom Misch|last=Casagrande|first=Tim|date=11 April 2018|website=SF Weekly|accessdate=16 September 2018}}</ref> and released his debut studio album ''[[Geography (Tom Misch album)|Geography]]'' in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.clashmusic.com/news/tom-misch-announces-debut-album-geography|title=Tom Misch Announces Debut Album 'Geography'|last=Murray|first=Robin|date=3 November 2017|website=Clash|accessdate=10 September 2018}}</ref> In 2020, he released his second studio album ''What Kinda Music'' in collaboration with English jazz drummer [[Yussef Dayes]],<ref>{{cite web |last1=Simon |first1=Scott |date=25 April 2020|title=Tom Misch On Making 'What Kinda Music' And Seeing Yussef Dayes At A Talent Show |url=https://www.npr.org/2020/04/25/844546201/tom-misch-on-making-what-kinda-music-and-seeing-yussef-dayes-at-a-talent-show |website=NPR |accessdate=2 May 2020}}</ref> distributed through [[Blue Note Records]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Skala |first1=Jemima |date=28 April 2020|title=What Kinda Music |url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/tom-misch-yussef-dayes-what-kinda-music/ |website=Pitchfork |accessdate=2 May 2020}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Misch studied music technology at [[Langley Park School for Boys]]<ref name=Billboard>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8263181/tom-misch-interview-geography|title=Tom Misch On Avoiding Jazz Snobbery and Why New School Hip-Hop Is 'Missing The Musicality'|last=Havens|first=Lyndsey|date=30 March 2018|website=Billboard|accessdate=10 September 2018}}</ref> and later, in 2014, enrolled in a jazz guitar course at [[Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance]] in [[Greenwich]] but left after six months to focus on his own music.<ref name=Standard>{{cite web|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/go/london/music/tom-misch-interview-the-idea-of-fame-really-scares-me-a3869761.html|title=Tom Misch interview: The idea of fame really scares me|last=Smyth|first=David|date=22 June 2018|website=London Evening Standard|accessdate=10 September 2018}}</ref> He began learning to play the violin at age 4, later learning to play the guitar.<ref name=NME>{{cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/music-interviews/tom-misch-loyle-carner-geography-2284028|title=Tom Misch: "Me and Loyle Carner definitely wanna do a little joint tape together"|last=Flood|first=Alex|date=15 April 2018|website=NME|accessdate=10 September 2018}}</ref>
