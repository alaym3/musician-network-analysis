'''Jean Carlos Santiago PÃ©rez''' (born September 16, 1992), known professionally as '''Guaynaa''', is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer. He is known for the song "ReBoTa", which became his first song to reach the ''Billboard'' [[Hot Latin Songs]] chart in April 2019.

He is signed to American music label [[Universal Music Latino]].

==Biography==
Guaynaa was born in [[Caguas, Puerto Rico]], to a Cuban mother and a Puerto Rican father. Before pursuing a musical career, he was working as a phone salesman in a mall.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.elnuevodia.com/entretenimiento/musica/notas/guaynaa-asegura-que-no-es-un-personaje/|title=Guaynaa asegura que no es un personaje|website=El Nuevo DÃ­a}}</ref> He was going to become a petrochemical engineer before working in music.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.abc.es/cultura/musica/abci-guaynaa-ingeniero-petroquimico-convirtio-revelacion-regueton-y-colaborador-mala-rodriguez-201910160929_noticia.html|title=Guaynaa, el ingeniero petroquÃ­mico que se convirtiÃ³ en revelaciÃ³n del reguetÃ³n y colaborador de Mala RodrÃ­guez|date=October 16, 2019|website=abc}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://elcomercio.pe/luces/musica/guaynaa-entrevista-responsable-exitoso-rebota-reggaeton-noticia-mamarre-ech1t-ecpm-678387-noticia/|title=MÃºsica: Guaynaa, el ingeniero quÃ­mico se convirtiÃ³ en la revelaciÃ³n del reggae &#124; NOTICIAS EL COMERCIO PERÃš|first=NOTICIAS EL COMERCIO|last=PERÃš|date=September 21, 2019|website=El Comercio PerÃº}}</ref>

==Career==
He gained fame due to the success of his song "ReBoTa", which reached number 35 on the ''Billboard'' Hot Latin Songs chart in April 2019 and has received over 300 million views on YouTube.<ref name=engineeringbillboard>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8542582/guaynaa-rebota-chartbreaker-interview|last=Lopez|first=Julyssa|title= Chartbreaker: Guaynaa Thought He'd Study Engineering, Then Landed a Stateside Hit|work=Billboard|date=November 5, 2019|accessdate=June 22, 2020}}</ref> The song's remix featured [[Becky G]], [[Farruko]], [[Nicky Jam]] and [[Sech (singer)|Sech]] in July 2019 and was later certified 4Ã— platinum by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]'s Latin division.<ref name=engineeringbillboard/> After the success of "ReBoTa", he signed a joint record deal with Universal Music Latino and Republic Records.<ref name=engineeringbillboard/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.noticel.com/fama/pop/musica/20191010/guaynaa-y-jacky-fontanez-se-separan/|title=Guaynaa y Jacky FontÃ¡nez se separan|website=www.noticel.com}}</ref>
