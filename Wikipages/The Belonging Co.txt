'''The Belonging Co''' is a [[contemporary worship music]] band founded by Henry Seeley, originating from The Belonging Co church in [[Nashville, Tennessee]].<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.allmusic.com/artist/the-belonging-co-mn0003675761/biography |title= The Belonging Co {{!}} Biography & History {{!}} AllMusic |last= Erlewine |first= Stephen Thomas |website= [[AllMusic]] |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> The collective has released three live albums, ''[[All the Earth]]'' (2017), ''[[Awe + Wonder]]'' (2019), and ''See the Light'' (2021).

==History==
The Belonging Co made its debut in 2017, released their first live album, ''[[All the Earth]]'', on September 8, 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.jubileecast.com/articles/18109/20170910/lauren-daigle-kari-jobe-mia-fieldes-offer-their-voices-to-the-belonging-cos-debut-album.htm |title= Lauren Daigle, Kari Jobe & Mia Fieldes Offer Their Voices to the Belonging Co's Debut Album : News : JubileeCast |last= Yap |first= Timothy |date= September 10, 2017 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> The album debuted on ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'''s [[Top Christian Albums Chart]] at number two, having sold 3,000 equivalent album units in its first week of sales.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/index.php/articles/business/chart-beat/7973554/tamela-mann-change-me-hot-gospel-songs-number-one |title= Tamela Mann Earns Second Hot Gospel Songs No. 1 {{!}} Billboard |last= Asker |first= Jim |date= September 21, 2017 |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= November 11, 2020 |quote= Nashville-based worship collective The Belonging Co arrives atop Billboard's Christian Album Sales chart and at No. 2 on the consumption-based Top Christian Albums list with its debut album, All the Earth. In its opening week, ending Sept. 14, Earth earned 3,000 equivalent album units, with 2,000 in traditional sales.}}</ref> The promotional single, "[[Peace Be Still (song)|Peace Be Still]]" featuring [[Lauren Daigle]], debuted at number 35 on the US [[Hot Christian Songs]], and spent a total of five weeks on the chart.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/music/the-belonging-co/chart-history/ICO/song/1045233 |title= Peace Be Still Chart History {{!}} Billboard |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |version= Hot Christian Songs |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref>

On March 23, 2018, The Belonging Co released "[[Isn't He (This Jesus)]]" featuring [[Natalie Grant]], as their first career single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/new-music/4685-the-belonging-co-releases-isn-t-he-this-jesus-featuring-natalie-grant |title= The Belonging Co Releases "Isn't He (This Jesus)" Featuring Natalie Grant |last= Longs |first= Herb |date= March 28, 2018 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> The song debuted at number 27 on the US [[Hot Christian Songs]] chart, and spent two weeks on the chart.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/music/the-belonging-co/chart-history/ICO/song/1071220 |title= Isn't He (This Jesus) Chart History {{!}} Billboard |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |version= Hot Christian Songs |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> On September 9, 2019, The Belonging Co released "Hosanna" featuring [[Kari Jobe]] as the first promotional single from their second live album, ''[[Awe + Wonder]]'', slated for release on September 13.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.multitracks.com/blog/the-belonging-co-hosanna |title= The Belonging Co - "Hosanna" |date= September 9, 2019 |website= MultiTracks |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.jubileecast.com/articles/22157/20190910/watch-the-belonging-cos-new-video-hosanna-featuring-kari-jobe-here.htm |title= Watch The Belonging Co's New Video "Hosanna" Featuring Kari Jobe Here : News : JubileeCast |date= September 10, 2019 |website= JubileeCast |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> ''Awe + Wonder'' was released on September 13, 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.multitracks.com/blog/new-album-from-the-belonging-co-awe-wonder |title= New Album from The Belonging Co. - Awe + Wonder |date= September 12, 2019 |website= MultiTracks |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> The album debuted at number 38 on the [[Top Christian Albums Chart]].<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/charts/christian-albums/2019-09-28 |title= Top Christian Albums Chart {{!}} Billboard |date= September 25, 2019 |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref>

On March 27, 2020, The Belonging Co released "Holy (Song of the Ages)" featuring Andrew Holt, as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/index.php/new-music/7389-the-belonging-co-s-holy-song-of-the-ages-echoes-eternal-hope-in-a-hurting-world |title= The Belonging Co's New Single Echoes Eternal Hope in a Hurting World |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= March 27, 2020 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= November 11, 2020}}</ref> On February 5, 2021, The Belonging Co released "[[Every Victory]]" featuring [[Danny Gokey]] as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/the-belonging-co-danny-gokey-release-every-victory/ |title= The Belonging Co Debut "Every Victory" Ft. Danny Gokey - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= February 5, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> "Every Victory" peaked at number 29 on the US Hot Christian Songs Chart.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.billboard.com/music/the-belonging-co/chart-history/ICO/song/1249046 |title= Every Victory Chart History {{!}} Billboard |website= [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |version= Hot Christian Songs |publisher= Billboard-Hollywood Reporter Media Group |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> On March 5, 2021, The Belonging Co released "Turn Your Eyes" featuring Natalie Grant as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/the-belonging-co-releases-turn-your-eyes-feat-natalie-grant/ |title= The Belonging Co Debuts "Turn Your Eyes" Feat. Natalie Grant - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= March 5, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> On April 2, 2021, The Belonging Co released "The Truth" featuring [[Fleurie (musician)|Lauren Strahm]] and Andrew Holt as a single.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/the-belonging-co-releases-turn-your-eyes-feat-natalie-grant/ |title= The Belonging Co Debuts "Turn Your Eyes" Feat. Natalie Grant - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= March 5, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> On May 7, 2021, The Belonging Co released "War Cry" featuring Henry Seeley as the first promotional single from ''See the Light'', their third live album was slated for release on May 21.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/the-belonging-co-releases-war-cry/ |title= The Belonging Co Releases New Single "War Cry" - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= May 7, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> On May 14, 2021, The Belonging Co released "Eyes on You" featuring [[Sarah Reeves]] as the second promotional single from ''See the Light''.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.thechristianbeat.org/the-belonging-co-releases-eyes-on-you-feat-sarah-reeves/ |title= The Belonging Co Releases "Eyes On You" Feat. Sarah Reeves - TCB |last= Clarks |first= Jessie |date= May 14, 2021 |website= The Christian Beat |access-date= May 17, 2021}}</ref> ''See the Light'' was released on May 21, 2021.
