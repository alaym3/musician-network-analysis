'''Danielle Peskowitz Bregoli''' (born March 26, 2003),<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8261436/bhad-bhabie-lil-yachty-gucci-flip-flops|title=Bhad Bhabie Celebrates 15th Birthday With New Song 'Gucci Flip Flops,' Feat. Lil Yachty|work=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=March 6, 2019}}</ref> known professionally as '''Bhad Bhabie''' ({{IPAc-en|b|√¶|d|_|'|b|e…™|b|iÀê}}, "bad baby"), is an American rapper, songwriter, and [[internet personality]]. She first became known from an appearance on ''[[Dr. Phil (talk show)|Dr. Phil]]'' in September 2016, in which she uttered the phrase, "Cash me outside, how bout dat?", which became a [[viral video]] [[meme (Internet)|meme]] and [[catchphrase]].<ref name=Wapo2017-02-12/><ref name="NYP">{{cite news|last1=Musumeci|first1=Natalie|title='Cash me outside' girl sentenced to 5 years probation|url=https://nypost.com/2017/08/02/cash-me-outside-girl-sentenced-to-5-years-probation/|publisher=The New York Post|date=August 2, 2017}}</ref> In 2017, Bregoli became the youngest female rapper ever to appear on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] chart with her debut single "[[These Heaux]]".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100/2017-09-23|title=Top 100 songs|website=Billboard.com|access-date=September 15, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/music/news/cash-me-outside-girl-signs-atlantic-records-contract-1202561216/|title='Cash Me Outside' Girl Signs With Atlantic Records: 'A Real Star With Undeniable Talent,' Says Label Executive|author=Variety Staff|date=September 16, 2017|work=Variety|access-date=February 26, 2018}}</ref> She subsequently signed a record deal with [[Atlantic Records]] and has since expanded into a reality show, a makeup brand, tours, and a music career. Bregoli released her first mixtape, ''[[15 (mixtape)|15]]'', in September 2018.
Its lead single, "[[Hi Bich]]", became her second single to appear on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100 chart.

===2016‚Äì2017: ''Dr. Phil'' appearance, rehab, and legal issues===
{{Redirect|Cash me outside|the related song by [[DJ Suede the Remix God]]|Cash Me Outside (song)}}

On September 14, 2016, Bregoli and her mother Barbara Ann were interviewed on ''[[Dr. Phil (talk show)|Dr. Phil]]'' for a segment titled "I Want to Give Up My Car-Stealing, Knife-Wielding, Twerking 13-Year-Old Daughter Who Tried to Frame Me for a Crime". They appeared on the show to discuss Bregoli's behavior, which included stealing a crew member's car while the episode was being filmed. When Bregoli became irritated at the audience's laughter, she responded to them by saying "Catch me outside, how about that?", jokingly challenging them to a fight outside the studio. Her accent made the phrase sound like "Cash me ousside, how bout dah", which became a meme, and Bregoli became known as the "'Cash Me Outside' Girl".<ref>{{cite news|last1=stubblebine|first1=allison|title=A Timeline of Danielle Bregoli's (a.k.a. the 'Cash Me Outside' Girl) Rise to Celebrity|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/7968844/danielle-bregolis-cash-me-outside-girl-timeline|access-date=February 11, 2018|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=September 19, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Desantis|first=Rachel|title=Cash me ousside teen to Dr. Phil ‚Äì You were nothing before me|url=http://ew.com/tv/2017/02/10/cash-me-ousside-danielle-bregoli-dr-phil/|publisher=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|date=February 10, 2017}}</ref><ref>Zimmerman, Amy (February 10, 2017). [[The Daily Beast]].</ref><ref>Wilson, Samantha (February 7, 2017). Bregoli and her mother sued three companies for "infringing her intellectual property rights" by using her signature catchphrase without consent. She also threatened to sue [[Walmart]] for using her catchphrase on T-shirts.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/danielle-bregoli-threatens-to-sue-walmart-2017-5|title=A 14-year-old girl who became famous overnight is feuding with Walmart|website=[[Business Insider]]|first=Hayley|last=Peterson|date=May 4, 2017|access-date=April 5, 2021}}</ref> She was nominated for the [[2017 MTV Movie & TV Awards]] in the [[2017 MTV Movie & TV Awards#Winners and nominees|"Trending"]] category based on the catchphrase.<ref>{{cite news|last=Lakshmin|first=Deepa|title=The "Cash Me Outside" Meme Is An MTV Movie & TV Awards Nominee, How Bout Dat?|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/3008390/mtv-movie-tv-awards-2017-trending-best-musical-moment/|publisher=MTV|date=May 2, 2017|access-date=September 23, 2017}}</ref>

Following her appearance on ''Dr. Phil'', Bregoli spent time at the Turn-About Ranch, a facility for "troubled teens" in [[Utah]]. She was later arrested and pleaded guilty to charges of grand theft, marijuana possession, and filing a false police report, for which she was sentenced to five years' probation in July 2017,<ref>Burke, Minyvonne (June 28, 2017). She accused staffers of forcing her to sit still for three days straight without being allowed to sleep, using physical restraint on other teenagers, and ignoring reports of bullying. She also criticized Dr. Phil for sending teenagers on his show to the facility.<ref name="VultureUtah">{{cite web|url=https://www.vulture.com/2021/03/bhad-bhabie-details-abuse-at-ranch-where-dr-phil-sent-her.html|title=Bhad Bhabie Details 'Abuse' at Utah Ranch Where Dr. Phil Sends Teens|website=[[Vulture (website)|Vulture]]|first=Rebecca|last=Alter|date=March 20, 2021|access-date=April 5, 2021}}</ref><ref name="RefineryUtah">{{cite web|url=https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2021/03/10379818/bhad-bhabie-teen-ranch-abuse-dr-phil|title=Bhad Bhabie Was Just 13 When Dr. Phil Sent Her To A Troubled Teen Ranch ‚Äî Here's What Happened|website=[[Refinery29]]|first=Elizabeth|last=Gulino|date=March 22, 2021|access-date=April 5, 2021}}</ref> He responded via an April 2021 interview with [[NewsNation]] that he is unaware of the ranch's actions and does not get feedback from them, to which Bregoli reacted by saying in a second video that the ranch sends progress reports directly to the show.<ref name="NewsweekUtah">{{cite web|url=https://www.newsweek.com/bhad-bhabie-calls-out-dr-phil-distancing-himself-turn-about-ranch-allegations-sexual-abuse-1581797|title=Bhad Bhabie Calls out Dr. Phil for Distancing Himself From Turn-About Ranch Allegations: 'Sexual Abuse, Murder, Torture'|website=[[Newsweek]]|first=Natalie|last=Colarossi|date=April 7, 2021|access-date=April 20, 2021}}</ref><ref name="BuzzfeedUtah">{{cite web|url=https://www.buzzfeed.com/ryanschocket2/dr-phil-responds-to-bhad-bhabie-allegations|title=Dr. Phil Has Responded To Bhad Bhabie's Allegations Of Abuse And Then She Replied With Another Video|website=[[BuzzFeed]]|first=Ryan|last=Shocket|date=April 10, 2021|access-date=April 20, 2021}}</ref>

===2019‚Äìpresent: ''Bringing Up Bhabie'', ''Ride or Die'' and return to music===

In January 2019, Bregoli released two singles: "Babyface Savage" featuring [[Tory Lanez]] and "Bestie" featuring [[Kodak Black]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8493059/bhad-bhabie-babyface-savage-video-tory-lanez|title=Bhad Bhabie & Tory Lanez Flex in Neon-Splashed 'Babyface Savage' Video: Watch|last=Saponara|first=Michael|date=January 10, 2019|website=Billboard|access-date=January 11, 2019}}</ref> Babyface Savage gained a lot of traction in YouTube and Twitter especially, with many clips of the songs gaining tens of thousands retweets and likes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/BhadBhabie/status/1083505266279104512|title=Babyface Savage Twitter snippet|website=Twitter.com}}</ref> The song also charted on Canada and on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 in the US, extending her record of the youngest female to have more entries in over 20 years.<ref name=":3" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2018/05/16/danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie-does-not-know-leann-rimes-billboard-chart-history/|title=Danielle Bregoli Matches a LeAnn Rimes Record, Has No Clue Who LeAnn Is|website=TMZ|language=en|access-date=2020-04-15}}</ref> The music video reached 20 million views in under 2 months. Bestie's music video, directed by Michael Garcia, came out February 25, and was praised for its visuals and storyline. It featured product placement by multiple brands, and had a guest appearance by [[DMX (rapper)|DMX]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bfmLYKxJ0UA| archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211113/bfmLYKxJ0UA| archive-date=2021-11-13 | url-status=live|title=Bestie Music Video|author=Bhad Bhabie|date=February 25, 2019|website=YouTube}}{{cbignore}}</ref>

In the same month, she announced an endorsement deal with Copycat Beauty, where she will promote the brand's products on her social media and music videos.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2019/01/16/danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie-makeup-endorsement-deal-copycat/|title=Danielle Bregoli Says Her Makeup Endorsements Could Rival Kylie Jenner's|website=TMZ|language=en|access-date=2020-04-15}}</ref> She reportedly will be making  a percentage of the sales for the six-month deal.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/01/bhad-bhabie-scores-900k-makeup-endorsement-deal|title=Bhad Bhabie Scores K Makeup Endorsement Deal|website=Complex|access-date=February 14, 2019}}</ref> The deal was successful, as the company's sales spiked, reaching 500,000 on the first day.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2019/01/21/danielle-bregoli-makeup-endorsement-deal-huge-sales-beauty-line-coming/|title=Danielle Bregoli's Makeup Endorsement Deal Already Bringing in Huge Sales|website=TMZ|language=en|access-date=2020-04-15}}</ref>

Bregoli also released the trailer of her new reality show, ''[[Bringing Up Bhabie]]'', which would detail her daily life and her building her music career. The show came out on [[Snapchat]], as one of their many Snap Originals. The first season had 12 episodes, and TMZ reported outstanding ratings of over 10 million unique viewers in its first 24 hours. They also reported she was set to make over 10 million dollars during 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2019/02/08/danielle-bregoli-snapchat-show-viewers-bringing-up-bhad-bhabie/|title=DANIELLE BREGOLI ‚Äì SNAPCHAT SERIES ON PACE TO SET RECORDS ‚Äì ... TV & Movie Roles Next?!?|date=Feb 2019|website=Tmz.com}}</ref> During September 2019, the reality show was renewed by Snapchat for a second season due to air in Summer 2020, but later delayed due to the [[COVID-19]] pandemic and cancelled later that year.

During April and May 2019, Bregoli released a remix of "Bestie" by Spenda C Nola Bounce, and a radio version of the song with [[Megan Thee Stallion]]. She released the version with Megan in order for her song to be played on the radio as radio stations were boycotting Kodak Black at the time. Bregoli also released the double singles "Lotta Dem" and her song "Spaz", which features [[YBN Nahmir]]. The making of the "Spaz" music video was recorded for her Snapchat show ''[[Bringing Up Bhabie]]''.<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/bhad-bhabie-lotta-dem-spaz-ybn-nahmir-841707/|title=Bhad Bhabie Is Back for Summer|first=Claire|last=Shaffer|magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=May 29, 2019}}</ref>

The following month, she released another single called "Get Like Me", which has a feature from [[NLE Choppa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Bhad Bhabie and NLE Choppa drop "Get Like Me" |url=https://www.thefader.com/2019/06/26/bhad-bhabie-and-nle-choppa-drop-get-like-me |website=The FADER |access-date=7 April 2020 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Bhad Bhabie's 'Get Like Me' Feat. NLE Choppa: Watch the Video |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8517909/bhad-bhabie-get-like-me-nle-choppa-video |website=Billboard |access-date=7 April 2020 |date=27 June 2019}}</ref> On August 22, 2019, she announced the release of her new game ''Ride or Die'', which features Bregoli as an animated character where the player has to get her to run from the police.<ref>{{cite web |last=Spangler |first=Todd |title=Bhad Bhabie Joyriding Game 'Ride or Die!' Geared Around Stealing Cars, Outrunning Cops |url=https://variety.com/2019/digital/news/bhad-bhabie-game-ride-or-die-bbtv-1203309629/ |website=Variety |date=22 August 2019}}</ref>

In December 2019, a video of Bregoli wearing [[box braids]] was posted to [[Instagram]], causing controversy over [[cultural appropriation]] and her subsequent decision to go on a social media hiatus.<ref>{{cite web |title=Bhad Bhabie claps back at criticism of box braids hairstyle |url=https://metro.co.uk/2019/12/04/bhad-bhabie-defends-box-braids-hairstyle-accused-cultural-appropriation-11267702/ |website=Metro |access-date=7 April 2020 |date=4 December 2019}}</ref> In February 2020, she ended her hiatus with a freestyle to [[Nicki Minaj]]'s song "[[Yikes (Nicki Minaj song)|Yikes]]",<ref>{{cite web |title=Bhad Bhabie Drops 'Yikes' Freestyle |url=https://www.rap-up.com/2020/02/11/bhad-bhabie-nicki-minaj-yikes-freestyle/ |website=Rap-Up |access-date=7 April 2020}}</ref> and another song called "$" with [[Lil Gotit]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Zidel |first1=Alex |title=Bhad Bhabie & Lil Gotit Link Up For "$" |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/bhad-bhabie-and-lil-gotit-link-up-for-s-new-song.1986405.html |website=HotNewHipHop |access-date=7 April 2020 |date=March 4, 2020}}</ref>
On April 22, 2020, she released her first single of 2020, titled "That's What I Said", a track addressing her critics.<ref name="Said">{{cite web|url=https://www.rap-up.com/2020/04/22/bhad-bhabie-thats-what-i-said/|title=Bhad Bhabie Confronts Her Critics on 'That's What I Said|work=[[Rap-Up]]|date=April 22, 2020|access-date=September 20, 2020}}</ref>

On March 17, 2020, [[Skai Jackson]] filed a restraining order against Bregoli following a heavily documented feud, in which Bregoli sent Jackson death threats and repeatedly harassed Jackson's mother on social media.<ref>https://www.insider.com/bhad-bhabie-danielle-bregoli-skai-jackson-feud-restraining-order-2020-3</ref><ref>https://www.eonline.com/news/1126953/breaking-down-skai-jackson-and-bhad-bhabie-s-feud-on-and-off-social-media</ref> Jackson dropped the restraining order on June 15.<ref>https://www.tmz.com/2020/06/15/skai-jackson-drops-restraining-order-danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie/ {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref>{{unreliable source?|reason=TMZ|date=September 2021}}

In June, Bregoli entered a 31 day Rehab Facility for childhood trauma and addiction to prescription medication.<ref>https://www.tmz.com/2020/06/03/danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie-enters-rehab-pills-trauma-treatment// {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref> She received support from celebrities such as [[Demi Lovato]], [[Blac Chyna]] and [[Charli XCX]].<ref name="tmz.com">https://www.tmz.com/2020/11/16/danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie-do-it-like-me-music-video-demi-lovato/ {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref>

In October, she released her second single of 2020, Do It Like Me, which quickly went viral on the social media platform, [[TikTok|Tik Tok]].<ref name="tmz.com"/>

On April 1, 2021, six days after her eighteenth birthday, Bregoli opened an [[OnlyFans]] account, earning over $1 million in revenue in the first six hours, including over $757,000 from subscriptions; $267,000 from message payments; and $5,000 in tips.<ref>
[https://headlineplanet.com/home/2021/04/01/bhad-bhabie-reports-over-1-million-in-onlyfans-account-earnings-after-just-six-hours/ Bhad Bhabie Reports Over $1 Million In OnlyFans Account Earnings After Just Six Hours] ''Headline Planet'', 2021/04/01</ref>

On April 4, [[TMZ]] reported that she and [[Paris Hilton]] were collaborating on a troubled teen program to focus on abuse experienced by teens at camps.<ref>https://www.tmz.com/2021/04/08/paris-hilton-team-up-danielle-bregoli-bhad-bhabie-troubled-teen/ {{Bare URL inline|date=November 2021}}</ref>

In September 2021, she launched her own record label, Bhad Music, where she will release her upcoming debut EP.<ref>https://variety.com/2021/music/news/bhad-bhabie-launches-label-masters-ownership-1235060700/</ref> On September 17, she released her first single of 2021, Miss Understood, which peaked at 8 on the Billboard Digital Song Sales Chart.<ref>https://www.billboard.com/music/bhad-bhabie/chart-history/HDS/song/1283738</ref> It is scheduled to be the lead single of her upcoming debut EP.<ref>https://news.yahoo.com/bhad-bhabie-releases-video-track-210635668.html</ref>
