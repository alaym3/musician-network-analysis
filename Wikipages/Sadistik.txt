'''Cody Foster''', better known by his [[stage name]] '''Sadistik''', is an American [[alternative hip hop]] artist from [[Seattle, Washington]]. He is currently unsigned to a label.

== Musical career ==
In 2008, he released his debut album ''The Balancing Act'' on Clockwork Grey Music.

In 2013, he released his sophomore LP ''[[Flowers for My Father]]'' on [[Fake Four Inc.|Fake Four Inc]], which received positive reviews.<ref>[https://www.sputnikmusic.com/review/55197/Sadistik-Flowers-For-My-Father/ Sputnik Music]</ref><ref>[http://www.syffal.com/sadistik-flowers-for-my-father SYFFAL (Magazine)]</ref><ref>[http://georgetownradio.com/album-review-sadistik-flowers-for-my-father Georgetown Radio] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303230709/http://georgetownradio.com/album-review-sadistik-flowers-for-my-father |date=2016-03-03 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.decoymusic.com/reviews/sadistik-flowers-for-my-father Decoy Music]</ref><ref>[http://potholesinmyblog.com/album-reviews-sadistik-flowers-for-my-father/ Potholes in my Blog]</ref> [[Vibe Magazine]] called the album "gripping & emotional".<ref>[http://www.vibe.com/article/v-premiere-sadistik-feat-cage-yes-alexander-russian-roulette-video VIBE V! Premiere]</ref> The album appeared on the top 15 hip-hop/rap charts on [[iTunes]].

On July 1, 2014, he released his third LP ''[[Ultraviolet (Sadistik album)|Ultraviolet]]''.

On April 14, 2017, he released his fourth LP ''Altars''.

On April 20, 2019, he released his fifth LP, ''Haunted Gardens''.

== Style ==

Sadistik has been described as "showing his scars with each song he writes"<ref>[http://ugsmag.com/2013/02/sadistik/ UGSMAG (Magazine)]</ref> and "the cigarette burn rap king".<ref>[http://www.thestranger.com/seattle/my-philosophy/Content?oid=16050686 The Stranger (Magazine)]</ref> Sadistik has been closely associated with the late [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] artist [[Eyedea]]<ref>[http://blogs.citypages.com/gimmenoise/2012/10/premiere_sadistik_releases_eyedea_tribute_micheal.php City Pages (Magazine)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140811034306/http://blogs.citypages.com/gimmenoise/2012/10/premiere_sadistik_releases_eyedea_tribute_micheal.php |date=2014-08-11 }}</ref> and has been known for "a unique style of hip hop, one that features dark imagery, dense wordplay, and nontraditional production styles."<ref>[http://scratchedvinyl.com/?q=node/726 Scratched Vinyl]</ref>

== Education ==

Sadistik went to [[Eisenhower High School (Yakima, Washington)|Eisenhower High School]] in [[Yakima, Washington]] and received a [[B.A.]] in both [[Psychology]] and [[Sociology]] at [[Central Washington University]].<ref>[http://www.xxlmag.com/rap-music/the-break/2013/04/the-break-presents-sadistik/ XXL The Break]</ref>

== Film ==

Sadistik's single, "Still Awake" will be featured in the upcoming short film, ''The Long Awake'', produced by Waverley Knobs Entertainment and starring Evin Charles Anderson
